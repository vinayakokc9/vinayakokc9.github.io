    • Return Policy

    1. Return Eligibility

    To return an item, your item must be unused and in the same condition it was upon delivery. All original packaging must be provided.

    2. Time Frame

    You have 30 days to return an item from the date the item was delivered.

    3. Refund Process

    Once we receive your item, we will send you an email. Then we'll notify you on the status of your refund as we process it.

    4. Shipping

    We'll cover the shipping costs for returning your item!

    If you have any questions on how to return your item, please contact our customer service team.
